Projects

Electronic Archive Training – Human Resources Department, Sharjah

Delivered a hands-on training program on implementing and managing an Electronic Archive System for the Human Resources Department of Sharjah. The training focused on digitizing records, secure document management, and improving internal workflows.
The program included real-world demonstrations of archiving systems, best practices for digital record-keeping, and practical sessions where HR staff engaged with a customized electronic archiving platform designed for their needs.

SABIS Advanced Robotics Competition – First Place Achievement

Our team proudly earned First Place in the SABIS Advanced Robotics Competition, showcasing excellence in design, innovation, and engineering. The project involved advanced robotics programming, strategic problem-solving, and collaborative teamwork. Through months of dedication and continuous refinement, we developed a high-performance robotic system that met complex technical challenges and demonstrated outstanding functionality during the competition.
This achievement highlights our commitment to pushing the boundaries of technology and innovation.

SABIS VEX Robotics Competition – First Place Award

Our team proudly achieved First Place at the VEX Robotics Competition, a prestigious event bringing together top talent in robotics design and engineering. We designed, built, and programmed a competitive robot that excelled in performance, strategy, and innovation.
This victory is a testament to our technical expertise, problem-solving skills, and strong teamwork. Winning this competition highlights our ability to deliver high-impact solutions in dynamic and challenging environments.
